How I Made $90 in 30 Days by Blogging: A Step-by-Step Guide

If you're looking for ways to make money online but don't know where to start? I was in the same boat until I decided to take action and try blogging for 30 days.

Surprisingly, I managed to earn $90 within that timeframe, and today I'll walk you through exactly how I did it.

Step 1: Research and Preparation

The first thing I did was research and select 30+ low competition keywords based on my desired niche. This helped me target specific topics that had the potential to attract traffic without facing too much competition.

Additionally, I purchased a cheap domain name from Hostinger for $0.99 and a hosting plan from Namecheap for $23 per year. Connecting everything was seamless, and I was ready to move on to the next step.

Step 2: Setting Up the Website

Once my domain and hosting were in place, I logged into my WordPress dashboard and installed necessary themes and plugins.

I spent time customizing the design, fonts, and typography to ensure a visually appealing website.

Remarkably, I managed to finish everything in just one day, setting a solid foundation for my blogging journey.

Step 3: Creating Content

With the design part out of the way, I dove into creating content.

Over the next few weeks, I wrote 70 blog posts focusing on various ways to generate passive income, including affiliate marketing, blogging, dropshipping, and selling digital products.

Each post was meticulously crafted to provide value and resonate with my target audience.

Step 4: Driving Traffic

After publishing my blog posts, it was time to attract visitors to my website. I worked on search engine optimization (SEO) to improve my site's visibility on Google and other search engines.

Additionally, I set up social media channels and started promoting my content to reach a wider audience.

Step 5: Monetization

To monetize my blog, I implemented multiple income streams.

I signed up for display ads to generate passive income from ad clicks.

I also promoted affiliate products related to my niche and earned commissions for every sale made through my referral links.
Additionally, I created and sold my own digital products, such as PDF guides, which proved to be a lucrative source of income.

Step 6: Results

Within the 30-day timeframe, I received my first online sales through selling my own products (PDF guides).
